About Paulo Coelho

Born in Rio de Janeiro in 1947, Coelho’s life was a journey of its own—from a rebellious youth to a world-renowned author. Published in 1988, The Alchemist became a global sensation, teaching millions that "when you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it."

The Plot

The Dream

Santiago, an Andalusian shepherd, dreams of treasure at the pyramids.

The Sacrifice

He sells his sheep and travels to Africa, where he is robbed and works for a crystal merchant.

The Desert

He joins a caravan, meets an Englishman, and eventually finds the Alchemist at an oasis.

The Revelation

He reaches the pyramids only to find that the treasure was at the church where he started.
